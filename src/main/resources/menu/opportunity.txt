An opportunity is a business possibility; is when a lead manifests interest to buy our products, generating an opportunity.

Here, you will find information like:
- a list of ids;
- the name of decision makers;
- the name and quantity of products ordered;
- the status of opportunity (if it is OPEN or CLOSED_LOST or CLOSED_WON)

Type:
1 - To view a specific Opportunity
2 - To view all Opportunities
3 - To set opportunity status
Type BACK to back the menu
Type EXIT to exit the CRM

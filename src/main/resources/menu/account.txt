An account is a document to find all the information that you need to close a deal.
Here is all the information of the Opportunity plus new information like the industry name, the city and the country where the industry is. All these dates generate an account.

Type:
1 - To manage a specific account
2 - To access all accounts
3 - To access a list of contacts
4 - To access a list of opportunities

Type BACK to back the menu
Type EXIT to exit the CRM
